This policy applies to the website of Meet Computer Education & Coaching Class, Boisar West, Palghar — a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We have no user accounts, no login, and no payment processing on this site.
Section 01

What We Collect

Our website is a simple static information page. We collect information in only one way — when you voluntarily fill in our contact/enquiry form. The fields you can submit are:

  • Your name — so we know who to respond to.
  • Your phone number — to call or WhatsApp you back.
  • Your email address — optional, if you'd prefer email replies.
  • Course interest — whichever course you select in the dropdown.
  • Your message — anything extra you type in the message box.
  That's it. We do not collect passwords, payment details, government IDs, location data, or any other sensitive information.
Section 02

How We Use Your Information

We use your form submission for one purpose only — to respond to your enquiry:

  • To call or WhatsApp you back about the course or coaching class you asked about.
  • To answer any questions you wrote in your message.

We do not send marketing emails, add you to any mailing list, share your details with advertisers, or use your information for any other purpose. We will never sell or rent your contact details.

Section 03

Web3Forms — Our Form Service

Our contact form is powered by Web3Forms (web3forms.com), a third-party form submission service. Here is exactly what happens when you hit "Send Message":

  • Your form data is sent securely (HTTPS) to Web3Forms' servers.
  • Web3Forms forwards the submission to our email inbox.
  • Web3Forms acts as a relay — it does not permanently store your submission data.
  • Once it arrives in our inbox, we read it and reply to you, then archive or delete it.
  Web3Forms has its own privacy policy at web3forms.com/privacy. We encourage you to review it to understand how they handle data in transit.
Section 04

GitHub Pages — Our Website Host

This website is hosted for free on GitHub Pages by GitHub, Inc. When you visit any page on our site, GitHub's servers automatically log some standard technical information:

  • Your IP address — logged by GitHub's infrastructure.
  • Browser & device type — standard server log data.
  • Pages visited — which files/pages your browser requested.

We do not have direct access to these server logs — they are held by GitHub. GitHub may retain them according to their own policies.

  For details on GitHub's data practices, see their Privacy Statement at docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies.
Section 05

Cookies & Tracking

Our website itself sets no cookies and uses no tracking or analytics scripts of any kind. However, two external services we use for fonts and icons may make requests from your browser to their servers:

  • Google Fonts — loads our typography (Syne & DM Sans). Google may log your IP when serving these font files, per Google's own privacy policy.
  • Font Awesome (via Cloudflare CDN) — loads our icons. Cloudflare may log your IP when serving these files, per Cloudflare's own privacy policy.
  No Google Analytics. No Facebook Pixel. No ad trackers. No remarketing. We keep it clean.
Section 06

Your Rights

Since we only hold the information you voluntarily sent us via the contact form, you have full and easy control:

  • Access — ask us what information we received from you.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your message and contact details from our inbox at any time.
  • Stop contact — simply tell us and we will not reach out to you again.

To exercise any of these, contact us using the details in Section 8. We are a small local institute and we will respond promptly.

Section 07

Policy Changes

If we ever update this policy — for example, if we add a new feature to the website — we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we have no user accounts, we cannot notify you directly. We recommend checking this page occasionally if you're interested.

  Current version: 10 March 2026. This reflects our site as it stands — a static GitHub Pages site with a Web3Forms contact form.
